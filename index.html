📖 Key Vocabulary — 2026 Theme
Special Area
Epistemic Impossibility
Something unknowable given the limits of human knowledge or logical systems — Gödel's incompleteness is the paradigm case.
Science
Quantum Superposition
A particle existing in multiple states simultaneously until observed — seemingly impossible by classical logic.
Social Studies
Paradigm Shift
Kuhn's term for a revolutionary change in the framework within which science or society operates.
Ethics
Moral Absolutism
The view that certain actions are intrinsically right or wrong regardless of consequences — making some ethical demands categorically impossible to satisfy.
Literature
Magical Realism
A literary mode in which impossible events are presented as ordinary — García Márquez's central technique.
Art
Avant-Garde
Movements that reject existing conventions in favour of radical experimentation — pursuing aesthetically impossible goals by conventional standards.
